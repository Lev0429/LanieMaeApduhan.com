About Me

Hi! I am Lanie Mae Renz Apduhan, an IT student at Davao Del Norte State College. The vast expanse of Information Technology is where I spend most of my time exploring. My focus is the continuous building of my skills and knowledge, which is necessary for me to be able to hold in the digital age. Innovation and problem-solving are the driving forces of my work, as I am forever in pursuit of improvements that can be made and taking advantage of our forever-growing technical abilities.

I play my part in the technological world by being part of the developers creating the latest trends and technologies. With the tools and creative means, we developed all these ideas into reality, and it assured us that the end-users could appreciate the ingenuity of the product. During weekends, I like to dive into discovering the most recent courses and projects in IT, build coding projects, and involved in the conceptual and development process with my peers to explore my knowledge.

A journey of discovery and growth as I am setting off on this wonderful road with the promise of doing my best and never stopping learning.

"Carmelita"

First

"Carmelita"

The poster for “Carmelita” effectively communicates domestic violence as the movie’s principal theme through strong visual and textual elements. It is a communication system that represents Carmelita’s pains and the presence of her overbearing husband, but also shows her determination and possibilities of hope as well as healing. The evocative images elicit interaction with Carmelita’s history, sympathizing with her predicament so that the movie becomes interesting or emotionally charged film to watch.

A successful poster of “Love Has Never Fades” greatly discusses unceasing love and emotional resistance in the film. NLP (NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING)

Third

NLP (NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of artificial intelligence focused on the interaction between computers and humans through natural language. It encompasses tasks like text analysis, which involves tokenization, parsing, and semantic analysis; sentiment analysis to determine emotional tone; language translation to convert text between languages while preserving meaning; speech recognition to convert spoken language into text; and text generation to produce human-like text from inputs. These processes are driven by various algorithms and models, including machine learning and deep learning techniques.

MOVIE LIST

First

MOVIE LIST

The user interface for adding a new movie to an application or database features a modern design with a blue film reel icon against a patterned background, symbolizing cinema. In the foreground, a form includes fields for Movie Title, Main Character, a dropdown menu for Movie Type, and Movie Description. Below the form are three buttons: Add Movie to submit the form, Back to return to the previous screen, and Rollback to undo the last action. The design is intuitive, making it easy to input movie data.
